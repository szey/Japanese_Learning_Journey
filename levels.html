Detailed map

What each stage should feel like.

Pre-N5

Foundation

Learn to read the scripts

  • Hiragana and Katakana
  • Pronunciation basics
  • Short reading chunks
  • First greetings and numbers
N5

Starter

Build the first grammar engine

  • です / ます patterns
  • Core particles
  • Basic verbs and adjectives
  • Very short dialogues
N4

Elementary

Handle daily topics

  • Verb transformations
  • Routine conversation
  • Longer listening chunks
  • More functional kanji
N3

Bridge

Cross into intermediate Japanese

  • Connected ideas
  • Notices and short articles
  • More natural rhythm
  • Themed vocabulary growth
N2

Advanced

Use Japanese more independently

  • Dense text reading
  • Subtle grammar differences
  • Fast listening under pressure
  • Serious mock exam work
N1

Mastery

Approach near-academic comprehension

  • Abstract writing and editorials
  • Rare expressions and nuance
  • High-speed reading strategies
  • Top-end refinement
